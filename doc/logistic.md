What are Logistics Facilities?
Responsible For Transporting Products
Logistics Facilities are structures that assist in transporting materials and products between major facilities.

Flavor Text
A basic conveyor belt for transporting things. Belts are the blood vessels of the AIC Factory.

For example, you can connect a Transport Belt from the Sub-PAC to a Refining Unit to process raw materials into valuable resources, then connect another Transport Belt leading back into the Sub-PAC to store your processed material.
